Stars Story

2020

In 2020, our company was founded with great aspirations and ambitions, fueled by a passion for providing exceptional forklift and Material Handling Equipment (MHE) solutions, we embarked on a journey to transform the industry, with a dedicated team of industry experts, we set out to establish ourselves as a trusted name in the market.

2021

This year marked an important milestone for our company. Building upon the foundation laid in the previous year, we focused on introducing new and advanced machinery into our product portfolio.

By forging strategic partnerships with renowned manufacturers, we ensured that our customers had access to the latest technology and innovative solutions for their material handling needs.

2022

Continuing our commitment to excellence, the year 2022 witnessed significant growth and expansion; we expanded our reach and diversified our customer base, serving businesses of all sizes and industries.

Through a customer-centric approach, we deepened our understanding of their unique requirements and provided tailored solutions to optimize their material handling operations.

2023

As we progress into 2023, our company remains dedicated to redefining the standards of the industry, with a focus on continuous improvement and innovation, we strive to stay ahead of the curve.

By investing in research and development and leveraging emerging technologies, we ensure that our customers benefit from the most advanced and efficient forklift and MHE solutions available.
